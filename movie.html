Movie Reviews

I will do some reviews of some of my favorite movies on this page. Don't worry. There's no spoilers.

Mitchells vs the Machines

Mitchells vs the Machines is a great movie that can be found on Netflix. The animation style is great, and it's surprisingly hilarious. It gets a 9.5/10!

Meet the Robinsons

Meet the Robinsons is probably my favorite time travel movie. Despite being a family friendly movie, it provides a great message and emotional moments. It gets a 9/10.

The Incredibles

The Incredibles is a classic Pixar movie about a superhero family. The movie jumps straight into the action and continues to roll the plot out and explain the world in unique ways. It gets a 9.25/10.
